Who We Are

PatentMuse is a Germany-based startup building an AI-powered semantic search engine for patents.

Our mission is to transform prior art search into a fast, accurate, and seamless experience. By combining artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and data privacy, we help lawyers, researchers, and companies discover in minutes what once took hours. We believe innovation deserves protection without unnecessary barriers. That’s why we design solutions that are simple, precise, and built for professionals in law and R&D.

Discover

Find what truly matters. With our AI-powered semantic search, you uncover relevant prior art in minutes instead of hours. No more endless keyword guessing. our technology understands meaning, context, and language to bring hidden insights to the surface.

Understand

Go deeper than words. PatentMuse analyzes concepts, synonyms, and translations across multiple languages, giving you a complete picture of the patent landscape. This ensures nothing slips through the cracks, even in the most complex cases.

Protect

Safeguard your ideas with confidence. Our platform is built with privacy and accuracy at its core all processing happens locally, ensuring your data stays secure while delivering results you can trust.

Recognition

PatentMuse is proud to be the winner of the AI Start Bash 4 program, organized by Campus Founders, one of Germany’s leading startup and innovation hubs. This recognition validates our vision and accelerates our journey, supported by a network of experts and mentors who believe in the future we are building.

Fast, Secure, and Intelligent Patent Search
