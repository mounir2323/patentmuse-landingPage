Who We Are

PatentMuse is a Germany-based startup building an AI-powered semantic search engine for patents.

Our mission is to transform prior art search into a fast, accurate, and seamless experience. By combining artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and data privacy, we help lawyers, researchers, and companies discover in minutes what once took hours.
